Old fashioned

Description

The old fashioned is a cocktail made by muddling sugar with bitters and water, adding whiskey (typically rye or bourbon), and garnishing with orange slice or zest and a cocktail cherry. It is traditionally served in an old fashioned glass (also known as rocks glass), which predated the cocktail.

Developed during the 19th century and given its name in the 1880s, it is an IBA Official Cocktail.[1] It is also one of six basic drinks listed in David A. Embury's The Fine Art of Mixing Drinks.

Ingredients

You will need the following:

  • Gin cocktail
  • small bar glas
  • 3 to 4 dashes of gump sirup
  • 1 or 2 dashes of Curacao
  • 1 small piece of lemon peel

mix it in the following steps:

  1. set up your bar glass and fill in the gin cocktail
  2. add 3 dashes of the sirup
  3. add 2 dashes of Curacao
  4. mix it
  5. at last add the piece of lemon peel on top
  6. enjoy:)
